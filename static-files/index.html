What we're about

In March 2012, Stephen Wolfram wrote an earth-shattering blog post entitled The Personal Analytics of My Life. His post shares the insights of over a decade of personal data analysis, using his email, calendar, keystroke data and a pedometer.

Wolfram demonstrates that data is powerful, if you know what to do with it. Data helps us predict the weather, build stock portfolios, and make the trains run on time. You could even say that data and data analysis tools allow us to perceive the world around us - light is just data interpreted by our eyes and brains.

Data can be world-changing, but we have far more data than we're able to effectively analyze. That's the main message we took from Wolfram's article: with better tools, we can turn data into insights in order to live better lives.

EmailCharts is just one step in that lifelong quest. Connect your email account, and we'll help you understand your email behavior - creating simple visualizations that reveal the patterns in your email data. At the end of the day, we have one simple goal: to help you understand how you work, so that you can improve how you live.
